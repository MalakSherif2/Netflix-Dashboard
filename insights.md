# 🎬 Netflix Content Insights

## 🔍 Overview

This dashboard analyzes Netflix content, focusing on movies, TV shows, ratings, release trends, and geographical distribution.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Movies: **6.13K**
* Total TV Shows: **2.67K**
* Total Directors: **6.17K**
* Total Countries: **7.97K**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

### 1. Movies Dominate Netflix Content

Movies significantly outnumber TV shows on Netflix, indicating a stronger focus on film-based content.

---

### 2. The United States Leads Content Production

The United States produces the highest number of releases by a large margin, followed by India and the United Kingdom.

---

### 3. Content Growth Peaked Around 2019–2020

* Netflix content saw rapid growth starting from 2016
* Peak production occurred around **2019 and 2020**
* Slight decline appears after 2020

---

### 4. Ratings Distribution Shows Family-Friendly Content

Top ratings such as:

* TV-PG
* TV-Y
* TV-Y7

indicate a strong presence of family and youth-oriented content.

---

### 5. Movies Are More Common Than TV Shows Over Time

The trend chart shows that movies consistently dominate over TV shows across the years.

---

## 🎯 Final Insight

Netflix’s strategy appears to prioritize:

* High-volume movie production
* Strong U.S.-based content
* Rapid expansion between 2016–2020

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

Netflix content strategy focuses on scaling movie production globally, while maintaining a diverse catalog across countries and audience age groups.
